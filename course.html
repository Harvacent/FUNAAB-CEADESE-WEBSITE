POSTGRADUATE COURSES.



The Centre of Excellence in Agricultural Development and Sustainable Environment (CEADESE) runs Specialised Postgraduate courses leading to award of Master (M.Sc/M.AgSE) and Doctorate (PhD/PhD.AgSE) degrees in Agricultural Development and Sustainable Environment. The courses are designed to integrate academic and entrepreneurial skills with demand-driven research. The Centre runs the following options:

Agricultural Economics and Environmental Policy

Agricultural Mechanization and Sustainable Environment

Crop and Pasture Production and Sustainable Environment

Environmental Systems and Climate Change

Food Processing and Value Addition

Livestock Science and Sustainable Environment

NO DISCRIMINATION.



Admissions into CEADESE programmes are based on regional and gender balance.
Non-nationals of Nigeria and Women are therefore encouraged to apply.
